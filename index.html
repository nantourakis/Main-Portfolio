About Me

Hi, I’m Nick Antourakis! I’m a Full Stack Developer who is based in Richmond, Virginia. I enjoy working within all aspects of software development and am always open to learning new skills. I’m a hard worker with an outstanding work ethic who loves seeing my team members and those around me succeed. Regardless of where I’m at in life, I never want to stop learning and will always strive to better myself.

I graduated from Virginia Commonwealth University in 2014 with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and Management. I have a background in operations and client servicing from working within the banking and financial industry. I have been Interested in programming since my early college days and have recently rekindled this passion. This led me to enroll into University of Richmond’s Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp where I earned my certification in 2018. Here I grasped a better understanding of web development by diving further into front and backend development, DOM manipulation, Restful API’s, & CRUD Methodologies

I love staying active, I can be found hitting the gym in the early hours before work and playing soccer or basketball over the weekend. When I’m not coding - I’m either exploring different restaurants, looking into nearby concerts, or dodging turtle shells while playing Mario Kart.

Recently I have been mainly developing in C# and am always striving to improve my skillset. I built my portfolio from scratch using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Any constructive criticism is appreciated - I hope you enjoy it!
